LEMON PASTA RECIPE

Description

Ingredients

  • 300 gr of pasta
  • zest of 1 lemon
  • 3 Tbs of olive oil
  • juice of 2 lemons

  1. STEP 1

    Boil the pasta, and heat up a sauce pan over medium heat then add the olive oil

  2. STEP 2

    add half the lemon juice to the olive oil and put the heat to high

  3. STEP 3

    Add the pasta to your olive oil mixture then add in the zest and the rest of the lemon juice

  4. STEP 4

    add some cheese to the pan and some pasta water and thats it enjoy
